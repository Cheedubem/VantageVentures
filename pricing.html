Investing is the act of committing funds to an asset or venture with the expectation of generating income or profit over time. It's a strategic allocation of resources aimed at achieving financial goals, whether it be wealth accumulation, retirement planning, or funding future endeavors. Investing involves assessing risk, conducting research, and making informed decisions to maximize returns while managing potential downsides.


Investments encompass a wide array of asset classes, including stocks, bonds, mutual funds, real estate, commodities, and alternative investments like cryptocurrencies or peer-to-peer lending. Each asset class carries its own risk-return profile and characteristics, catering to diverse investor preferences, goals, and risk tolerances.
